Frequently Asked Questions

What information do you need for a capability assessment?

We'll need details about your operational environment, soldier population (male/female/mixed), specific performance requirements, timeline, and estimated quantities. The more detail you provide, the faster we can respond with relevant solutions.

Do you support custom design and development?

Yes. We specialize in custom programs from concept through full-scale production. Our NRE (Non-Recurring Engineering) process accommodates unique requirements, materials, fit specifications, and integration needs.

What are your standard lead times?

Standard programs typically require 8-12 weeks from order to delivery. We can support expedited timelines (4-6 weeks) for urgent requirements, subject to production capacity and material availability.

Are your products compliant with military standards?

Yes. Our tactical apparel meets or exceeds NATO STANAG specifications, Canadian Armed Forces standards, and US Military Specifications (MIL-STD). All products undergo rigorous testing and compliance verification.

Do you support classified procurement?

We have processes in place for handling sensitive government inquiries and can discuss cleared personnel capacity and data handling protocols directly. Contact our team for confidential discussion.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Standard MOQ depends on product type and customization. For high-volume products, MOQs may be 500-1,000 units. Custom programs can accommodate smaller quantities (100+). Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

Can you accommodate female-specific fit?

Absolutely. We specialize in gender-specific engineering and have proven experience delivering female-optimized uniforms at scale (50,000+ units to Ukraine). This is a core capability.

Do you offer volume pricing discounts?

Yes. We offer volume-based pricing for large programmes. Pricing varies based on customization level, materials, and order volume. We'll provide detailed quotes based on your specific requirements.

Are your products sustainable/eco-friendly?

We're committed to sustainability (B-Corp certified, ISO 14001). We offer sustainable material alternatives for many products, though we don't compromise on performance. Discuss your sustainability requirements with our team.

How do I submit a formal RFP to Wuxly?

Contact our business development team directly via email or phone. We'll discuss your RFP requirements and provide relevant documentation, capability statements, technical data sheets, and compliance matrices needed for your evaluation.
