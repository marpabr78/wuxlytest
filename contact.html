Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical lead time?

Standard programmes: 8-12 weeks. Expedited: 4-6 weeks. Rapid prototyping: 2-4 weeks.

Do you support classified procurement?

Yes. We have processes for sensitive government inquiries with cleared personnel and secure protocols.

What are your minimum order quantities?

Varies by product. High-volume: 500-1,000 units. Custom: 100+ units. Contact us for your specific needs.

Do uniforms meet NATO standards?

Yes. NATO STANAG, Canadian Armed Forces, and US Military Specifications. All undergo rigorous testing.

Do you offer female-specific fit?

Absolutely. Gender-specific engineering is a core capability with proven delivery records.

What is your manufacturing capacity?

50+ partners across Canada with 10M+ units produced. Substantial capacity for large programmes.

Do you offer volume pricing?

Yes. Volume-based pricing for large programmes. Detailed quotes provided based on specifications.

Are products sustainable?

Yes. B-Corp certified (ISO 14001). Sustainable material alternatives available without performance compromise.

How do I submit an RFP?

Contact our team. We'll provide capability statements, technical data sheets, and compliance matrices.

What is your on-time delivery record?

100%. Proven track record of consistent, reliable execution against committed timelines.
