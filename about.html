About Wuxly Defence

Canadian-engineered textile solutions for extreme cold and tactical operations, trusted by NATO and allied defence forces.

Our Story

Since 2015: Engineering Excellence in Canada

Wuxly has engineered high-performance textile solutions designed to meet the demands of extreme conditions. Proudly made in Canada, our outerwear and uniform systems combine technical innovation with ethical manufacturing.

Global Trust

Our products are trusted by NATO member nations, Canada, the USA, and EU defence organizations. We're proud to be an approved supplier to leading military organizations.
