About Wuxly Defence

Canadian-owned innovation driving precision textile solutions for global defence and tactical operations.

Our Mission

Wuxly Defence is committed to delivering mission-critical textile solutions to government, defence, and tactical organizations worldwide. Built on principles of innovation, integrity, and Canadian excellence, we engineer advanced apparel and smart textile systems that empower operators in the world's most demanding conditions.

A Vision Takes Shape

The Wuxly movement ignited in 2012 when pro-football player James Yurichuk relocated from temperate British Columbia to Toronto's harsh winters. Searching for a high-performance parka for his girlfriend Daniela, newly arrived from Brazil, James found nothing that met his exacting standards for quality, ethics, and sustainability‚Äîprompting him to forge his own path.

Determined to create a solution, James partnered with master tailor Anthony Debartolo, leveraging Canada's rich manufacturing heritage and sustainable fabrics to craft the first Wuxly parka. This marked the beginning of a legacy‚ÄîCanadian-made outerwear designed to deliver warmth, durability, and purpose, both in function and in values.

Leadership & Vision

James Yurichuk, CEO & Founder

Drafted 4th overall by the BC Lions in 2009, James showcased leadership and grit, contributing to a Grey Cup win that year. After joining the Toronto Argonauts in 2013, his on-field tenacity now fuels his strategic vision as Wuxly's leader.

In 2014, James earned the Jake Gaudaur Veterans' Award, recognizing his embodiment of Canadian veterans' values. His commitment to community shines through initiatives like anti-bullying campaigns, volunteer coaching, and advancing women's football leagues.

A Lasting Legacy

Daniela's love for the parka sealed its success. James and Daniela later married and now raise their four children in Toronto, where Wuxly continues to thrive as a beacon of innovation and integrity in the textile industry.

What began as one person's quest to solve a personal problem has evolved into a global enterprise serving government, defence, and tactical organizations across NATO and allied nations.

Our Commitments

Ethical Labour Practices

At Wuxly, we uphold an unwavering commitment to ethical labor practices, ensuring everyone in our production process thrives in safe, fair, and dignified conditions that meet the highest standards of labour ethics. We believe that quality products begin with respected people and dignified work.

B-Corp Certified

We proudly hold B Corp certification, reflecting our dedication to balancing purpose and profit. This standard fuels our commitment to sustainable, ethical, and transparent practices, ensuring our textile solutions deliver exceptional quality while creating positive social and environmental impact.

Indigenous Relations

We actively foster respectful and collaborative partnerships with Indigenous communities, honouring their rich heritage in our practices while contributing to their economic empowerment through collaborative opportunities and supply chain participation.

Sustainability & Textiles

We strategically source advanced textiles engineered to meet rigorous performance criteria while adhering to uncompromising sustainability protocols. Every material is meticulously selected for optimal durability, functionality, and minimal ecological footprint, ensuring tactical reliability and comfort in the most demanding conditions.

Net Zero Challenges

We are proud participants in Canada's Net Zero Challenge, committing to achieve net-zero emissions across our scope 1, 2, and applicable scope 3 operations by 2050, in alignment with the Canadian Net-Zero Emissions Accountability Act. Through innovative, sustainable textile solutions, we're helping Canada meet its 2030 emissions reduction goals.
