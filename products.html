Sleeping Systems

Extreme Cold Sleeping Bags

Engineered for prolonged exposure in sub-zero environments with military-grade construction and thermal performance ratings.

Extreme Cold Sleeping Bag (ECSB)

Thermal Performance Ratings:

  • Comfort: -10°C (15°F)
  • Limit: -17°C (1°F)
  • Extreme: -39°C (-38°F)

Construction & Materials:

  • Climashield® Combat-A 3.6 oz continuous filament insulation
  • Berry Amendment compliance
  • ≥1.95 CLO rating (before laundering)
  • 90% compression recovery
  • Minimal shrinkage (<0.5%) after laundering

Design Features:

  • Anatomical hood and footbox for core warmth
  • Ergonomic mummy shape
  • Adjustable drawcord closures
  • Optimized insulation zones

Specifications:

  • Available sizes: Medium, Large, Extra-Large (up to 76" stature)
  • Large dimensions: 82" L x 35" W
  • Weight: 2.94 kg (6.5 lbs)
  • Packed volume: 27.76 L
  • Water resistant outer shell: 70D nylon ripstop in Canadian Green
  • Tear strength exceeds military requirements
